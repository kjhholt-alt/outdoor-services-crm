# AATOS CRM - Client Handover Guide

Welcome to your new CRM! This guide will help you get started managing your outdoor services business.

## Getting Started

### 1. First Login
- Go to your CRM URL (provided separately)
- Log in with the credentials we set up for you
- You'll see the **Dashboard** with today's jobs, revenue stats, and reminders

### 2. Set Up Your Company Info
- Go to **Settings** (bottom of the left sidebar)
- Update your business name, address, phone, and email
- Set your default tax rate for invoices
- Set your payment terms

### 3. Import Your Customer List
If you have an existing customer list in Excel or CSV:
1. Go to **Import/Export** in the sidebar
2. Click the upload area and select your file
3. Map your columns to CRM fields (name, phone, email, address)
4. Choose how to handle duplicates
5. Click **Import**

## Daily Workflow

### Morning Routine
1. **Check Dashboard** - See today's jobs, any overdue invoices, and reminders
2. **Review Crew View** - Click "Crew View" for a mobile-friendly list of today's jobs with addresses and times
3. **Print/share the daily schedule** - The crew view shows jobs in time order with customer addresses and phone numbers

### During the Day
1. **Mark jobs as started** - Tap "Start" when arriving at a job site
2. **Mark jobs as complete** - Tap "Complete" when finished
3. **Add photos** - Use the camera icon to capture before/after photos

### End of Day
1. **Create invoices** - Go to Invoices > New Invoice, select the customer, attach completed jobs
2. **Download PDF** - Click the download icon to get a professional PDF invoice
3. **Mark invoices paid** - Click the checkmark icon when payment is received

## Key Features

### Customers
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find customers by name, email, or phone
- **Notes**: Add notes to customer records - they're timestamped and versioned
- **Quick Call**: Tap any phone number to call directly from your phone

### Jobs
- **Calendar View**: Switch to "Calendar" tab to see jobs on a monthly/weekly calendar
- **Color Coding**: Jobs are color-coded by service type (green = lawn care, blue = snow, etc.)
- **Status Flow**: Scheduled → In Progress → Completed

### Invoices
- **Create from Jobs**: Select completed jobs to auto-populate invoice amounts
- **Tax Calculation**: Tax is calculated automatically based on your settings
- **PDF Download**: Professional PDF invoices with your company info
- **Payment Tracking**: Mark invoices as paid with the checkmark button

### Estimates
- **Create Quotes**: Add line items with descriptions and prices
- **Accept to Create Jobs**: Click "Accept" on a sent estimate to automatically create jobs from it
- **Track Status**: Draft → Sent → Accepted/Declined

### Routes
- **Plan Routes**: Select customers to visit, and the system optimizes the driving order
- **Track Progress**: Mark stops as completed during the day

### Global Search
- Press **Ctrl+K** (or click the search bar) to search across all customers, jobs, and invoices

## FAQ

**Q: How do I add a new service type?**
A: Go to Settings > Service Types. Click "Add" at the bottom to create a new service.

**Q: How do I change the tax rate?**
A: Go to Settings > Invoice Settings. Update the "Default Tax Rate" field.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
A: Yes! The CRM is fully mobile-responsive. The "Crew View" is especially designed for phone use in the field.

**Q: How do I delete a customer?**
A: From the Customers list, click on the customer, then use the delete option. Note: customers are "soft deleted" - their data is preserved but hidden from lists.

**Q: What happens to demo data?**
A: The sample customers, jobs, and invoices are real database records. You can delete them individually or keep them for reference. New records you create will appear alongside them.

**Q: Can multiple people use this at the same time?**
A: Yes! Each person needs their own login. Contact us to set up additional accounts.
